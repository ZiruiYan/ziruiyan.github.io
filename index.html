Bio

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in Department of Electrical, Computer, and System Engineering at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute (RPI), working under the supervision of Prof. Ali Tajer. Before that, I obtained my B.S. in Statistics and dual degree in Computer Science from University of Science and Technology of China in 2020.

My current research topic is causal bandit (see this github repo for brief summary) and the causality in Large Language models (LLM). I also have broad interests in the application of bandit in federated learning.
